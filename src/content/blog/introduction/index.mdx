### 🔐 ZenAuth
A **lightweight OAuth2 server written in Go** – designed as a clean and simple alternative to Keycloak.

**Features:**
- JWT and refresh tokens  
- PKCE support  
- Role & scope-based access  
- Admin UI & login screen theming  
- Microservice-ready and easy to self-host

Built for developers who want auth that works without the bloat.

### 🌍 RedoKube
A **Kubernetes-native OpenAPI hub** for discovering and visualizing API specs across your infrastructure.

**Features:**
- Auto-discover OpenAPI endpoints in services  
- Visualize them in a unified dashboard  
- Integrate versioning & changelogs  
- Share docs across your team without extra tooling

Great for internal developer platforms or API-first environments.
